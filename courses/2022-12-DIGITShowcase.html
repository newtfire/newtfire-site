DIGIT Works Project Showcase

December 9, 2022: 11am - 1pm

Location: Virtual (Zoom): Register for the Zoom link

Students present projects made individually or in teams in the following Fall 2022 classes at Penn State Erie, The Behrend College: DIGIT 110 (Text Encoding), DIGIT 400 (Digital Project Design), COMM 242 (Video Editing), GAME 180N (The Art and Science of Virtual Worlds), and GD 115N (Visualizing Information), taught by Profs. Elisa Beshero-Bondar, Tommy Hartung, and Lisa Jo Elliott.
