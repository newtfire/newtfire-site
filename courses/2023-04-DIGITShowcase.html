DIGIT Works Project Showcase

April 28, 2023: 12pm - 3:30pm

Location: Virtual (Zoom): Register for the Zoom link

DIGIT students from Penn State Erie, The Behrend College present their semester and senior design projects. The projects represent work in progress, nearing completion from Large-Scale Text Analysis (DIGIT 210) and Advanced Digital Creations (DIGIT 409) taught by Profs. Elisa Beshero-Bondar and Tommy Hartung.
