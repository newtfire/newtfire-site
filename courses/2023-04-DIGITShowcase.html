DIGIT Works Project Showcase

April 28, 2023: 12pm - 3:30pm

Location: Virtual (Zoom): Register for the Zoom link

DIGIT students from Penn State Erie, The Behrend College present their semester and senior design projects. The projects represent work in progress, nearing completion from Large-Scale Text Analysis (DIGIT 210), INART 258A (Fundamentals of Digital Audio), Advanced Digital Creations (DIGIT 409), and Technical Game Development (Game 250) taught by Profs. Elisa Beshero-Bondar, Joel Hunt, Tommy Hartung, and Pulin Agrawal.

This is an approximate schedule. Presentation times slated here may shift as we add more detail before the event or as presentations run a little longer or shorter than anticipated.
