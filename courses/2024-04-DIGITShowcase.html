DIGIT Works Project Showcase

April 26, 2024: 11am - 4:45pm

Location: Virtual (Zoom): Register for the Zoom link

DIGIT students from Penn State Erie, The Behrend College present their semester and senior design projects. The projects represent work in progress, nearing completion from Digit 494 Senior Capstone projects, Advanced Digital Creations (Digit 409), Data Visualization (Digit 410), Game Development Projects (Game 480), ATechnical Game Development (Game 250), Large-Scale Text Analysis (Digit 210), and Electronic Music Composition (Music 458) taught by Profs. Tommy Hartung, Lisa Jo Elliott, Lauren Liebe, Pulin Agrawal, Elisa Beshero-Bondar, and Joel Hunt.

This is an approximate schedule. Presentation times slated here may shift as we add more detail before the event or as presentations run a little longer or shorter than anticipated.
