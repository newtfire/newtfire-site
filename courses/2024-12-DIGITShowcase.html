DIGIT Works Project Showcase

December 13, 2024: 10am - 1:30pm

Locations:

  • In Person: Reed Union Building Auditorium (Room 117)
  • Virtual (Zoom) Register for the Zoom link

DIGIT students from Penn State Erie, The Behrend College present their semester and senior design projects. The projects represent work in progress, nearing completion from DIGIT 110 (Text Encoding), Photo 202 (Professional Photography), Digit 400 (Digital Project Design), 494 senior projects, Game Development Projects (Game 480) taught by Profs. Tommy Hartung, Lauren Liebe, Pulin Agrawal, Elisa Beshero-Bondar, and Joel Hunt.

This is an approximate schedule. Presentation times slated here may shift as we add more detail before the event or as presentations run a little longer or shorter than anticipated.
