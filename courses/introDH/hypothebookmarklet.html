Hypothes.is in Firefox: How to Set up the Bookmarklet

  • On the Hypothes.is Get Started page, look in step 2 (where it says Add Hypothesis to your browser).
  • Find the link marked Hypothes.is Bookmarklet. Right-click on the link and add it to the bookmarks for your browser
  • Open one of our readings in Firefox.
  • Find and open the Bookmarks in your Firefox, and find the Hypothes.is Bookmarklet. Click on it, and you will open the Hypothes.is controls so you can annotate.
