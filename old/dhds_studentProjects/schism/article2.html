Summary

This spilt affected the parishioners in the Pittsburgh area. This article asks whether or not the split changed peoples church life. Some felt the need to move parishes but still felt an attachment to their old church communities. Others felt a loss of community choosing to keep quiet, worship quietly, and keep to their own opinions. In most of the cases however the parishes remain friendly with each other despite the theological split. As for those who had a strong opinion about the split. This secession allowed for those parishioners to be able to speak their opinion. The split has offered many conflicting opinions on the subject.
