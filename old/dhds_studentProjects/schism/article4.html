Summary

The conservative half of the Episcopal Church of Pittsburgh chose to secede from the main part of the church to join another communion. The seceding half will still have the name "Episcopal Diocese of Pittsburgh," but it has added "of the Anglican Province of the Southern Cone." The province is located in Africa which has shocked many.
